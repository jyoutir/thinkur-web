questions

Frequently asked

Is it really free?

Yes. MIT license. No accounts, no tracking, no word limits. Free forever.

Does my audio ever leave my Mac?

No. Everything runs on-device via Apple's speech framework. Works fully offline.

How is this better than Apple Dictation?

thinkur removes filler words, formats numbers, adapts writing style per app, records meetings with speaker labels, and provides transcripts for AI tools. Apple Dictation does none of that.

Which Macs are supported?

Any Mac with Apple Silicon (M1 or later) running macOS 15 Sequoia or newer.

How does this compare to Wispr Flow?

Wispr Flow costs $12/month and sends your audio to the cloud. thinkur is free, open-source, and 100% local. Same quality, zero cost, total privacy.

Is it safe for sensitive documents?

Your audio and transcripts never leave your machine. No cloud. No analytics on your content. Your data is yours.
